## ABOUT PAGE

### School Overview
Future Minds Academy of Mandaluyong City, Inc. (FMA) is a DepEd-recognized private school serving families in Mandaluyong since 2005. For over two decades, we have been dedicated to providing quality early childhood and elementary education that builds strong foundations for lifelong learning.

### Our Legacy
- **Founded:** 2005
- **20th Anniversary:** Celebrated in 2025
- **School Head:** Mr. Orlando Francisco Fumera Jr.

### Affiliations & Recognition
- **CEdNet Member** - Christian Educators' Network
- **NATCON 5 Participant** - "Living the Virtues, Leading the Future"
- **DepEd NCR Recognized** - Annual permit renewal maintained

### Our Values
As a member of the Christian Educators' Network (CEdNet), FMA is committed to values-based education. Our participation in NATCON 5 with the theme "Living the Virtues, Leading the Future" reflects our dedication to nurturing not just academic excellence, but character development.

## EVENTS & NEWS PAGE

### Recent Highlights

#### Goldenberg Concert at Malacañang (November 2025)
FMA students had the amazing opportunity to attend the Goldenberg Concert Series XX at the historic Goldenberg Mansion in Malacañang. The PCSO Vocal Ensemble, under conductor Robert Delgado, performed Filipino folk music including "Leron Leron, Sinta" and contemporary favorites.

*Featured in: Manila Bulletin, GMA News*

#### 20th Commencement & Moving Up Ceremony (2025)
FMA celebrated 20 years of shaping young minds with our milestone graduation ceremony, honoring completers from preschool through Grade 6.
