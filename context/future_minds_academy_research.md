## SUMMARY

**Future Minds Academy of Mandaluyong City, Inc.** is a DepEd-recognized private school founded approximately in 2005, now celebrating 20 years of operation. The school offers Preschool (Nursery/Kindergarten) and Complete Elementary education (Grades 1-6). Located at the ground floor of Ma. Isabel Building on Boni Avenue in Barangay Plainview, Mandaluyong City, the school is led by Mr. Orlando Francisco Fumera Jr.

The school maintains an active presence through its Facebook page and participates in community and cultural events, including the prestigious Goldenberg Concert Series at Malacañang. It follows the DepEd K-12 curriculum, offers summer classes, and serves families in the Mandaluyong area seeking elementary education for their children.

### Key Highlights
- **20 years in operation** (2005-2025)
- **DepEd RECOGNIZED** status with annual permit renewal
- **Complete Elementary** program (Nursery to Grade 6)
- **Active community participation** in cultural and city events
- Located near **Boni MRT Station** for accessibility
